Coffee Biscuits

Description

"A buttery biscuit with a subtle coffee flavor." - Eorzean Database

  • Prep time: 1 hour and 30 minutes
  • Cook time: 20 minutes
  • Yields 18 large cookies

Ingredients

Cookies

  • 1 cup (227 g) butter
  • 1/2 cup (50 g) whole roasted coffee beans
  • 1 cup (200 g) sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp (5 mL) vanilla extract
  • 3 cups (525 g) all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup (100 g) cornstarch
  • 1 tsp (6 g) kosher salt

Caramel Topping

  • 1/4 cup (60 mL) water
  • 2 tbsp (15 g) finely ground coffee
  • 1 cup (200 g) sugar
  • 1/2 cup (125 mL) water
  • 3/4 cup (177 mL) heavy cream
  • 1/4 tsp (1.25 mL) vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp (1.5 g) kosher salt
  • 2 tbsp (30 g) butter

Steps

Cookies

  1. Put the butter in a small pot over low heat and let it melt completely.
  2. Add the coffee beans and let them soak in the butter for 1 hour over the lowest heat setting. More time will yield a stronger coffee flavor.
  3. Strain the coffee beans from the butter and discard them.
  4. Put the coffee butter in the refrigerator to cool and solidify.
  5. In a large bowl, cream together the sugar and the coffee butter until they are light and fluffy.
  6. Add the eggs and vanilla and mix until they are fully combined.
  7. Add the flour, cornstarch, and salt and mix until a crumbly dough forms.
  8. Separate the dough into 2 to 4 balls, depending on the size of your work surface.
  9. Place a ball of dough between two pieces of parchment paper and roll it out to 1/4 in or 6.5 mm thick.
  10. Use a cook cutter to cut out shapes and transfer them to a parchment paper lined baking sheet.
  11. Chill the cut dough in the freezer for 10 minutes.
  12. Preheat the oven to 325 F (163 C).
  13. Bake the cookies for 6 minutes, rotate and switch the pan positions, then bake for another 6 minutes.
  14. Let the cookies cool completely before decorating.

Caramel Topping

  1. Brew the ground coffee in 1/4 cup (60 mL) of water and set aside to cool.
  2. In a small pot set over low heat, combine the sugar and 1/2 cup (125 mL) of water. Allow the sugar to dissolve completely.
  3. Set the heat to medium, cover the pot with a lid, bring it to a boil, and let the solution cook for 5 to 10 minutes or until it becomes golden brown.
  4. Add the cream and whisk until it is fully combined.
  5. Add the coffee, vanilla, and salt, and whisk until they are fully combined.
  6. Take the pot off the heat, add the butter, and stir until it is fully melted.
  7. Allow the caramel to cool at least 1 hour before using it.
  8. Decorate the cookies with the caramel.
