Grilled Corn Salad

Description

Perfect for those who can't get behind the leafy vegetable craze, this grilled corn salad adds a beautiful pop of color to any lunch spread. It's perfect as a side, main course, or just a snack, and it goes great with any grilled dish. The best part, it can last several days when stored in an airtight, refrigerated container, so you can munch on it for days to come!

Ingredients

  • 6 ears freshly shucked corn
  • 1 green pepper, diced
  • 2 Roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
  • ¼ cup diced red onion
  • ½ bunch fresh cilantro, chopped, or more to taste
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil, or to taste
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Prepare an outdoor grill for medium heat.
  2. Lightly oil the grate.
  3. Cook the corn on the preheated grill, turning occasionally, until the corn is tender and specks of black appear, about 10 minutes.
  4. Set the corn aside until just cool enough to handle.
  5. Slice the kernels off of the cob and place into a bowl.
  6. Combine the warm corn kernels with the green pepper, diced tomato, onion, cilantro, and olive oil.
  7. Season with salt and pepper.
  8. Toss until evenly mixed.
  9. Set aside for at least 30 minutes to allow flavors to blend before serving.
